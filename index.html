Autismus--ADHS

ADHS means --attention deficit hyperactivity disorder

Autism syndrome is a disorder in perception processing. Environmental stimuli for the eye, ear or sense of touch cause problems for those affected - they are "overrun" by sensory impressions. As a further consequence, there are difficulties in social interaction.
